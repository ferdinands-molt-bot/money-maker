Getting Started

What is ContentMultiplier?
ContentMultiplier is an AI-powered tool that transforms your blog posts and articles into optimized content for multiple social media platforms. Simply paste your content, select the platforms you want, and get platform-specific posts in seconds.
How do I get started?
  1. Go to our app
  2. Paste your blog post or article
  3. Select the platforms you want content for
  4. Click "Generate" and get your content
No signup required for the free plan!
Is it really free?
Yes! Our free plan includes 3 conversions per month for Twitter and LinkedIn. For unlimited conversions and all 6 platforms, upgrade to Pro for just $9/month.

Features & Usage

Which platforms are supported?
We support 6 major platforms:
  • Twitter/X
  • LinkedIn
  • Instagram
  • Facebook
  • YouTube
  • Newsletter/Email
How does the AI work?
Our AI analyzes your content, extracts key points, and restructures them following platform-specific best practices. It considers optimal character counts, formatting styles, hashtag strategies, and engagement patterns unique to each platform.
Can I customize the output?
Yes! You can choose from multiple tones (professional, casual, enthusiastic, educational, witty) and edit the generated content before publishing. The AI gives you a strong starting point that you can refine to match your voice.
Is the generated content original?
Yes! The AI creates unique content based on your input. It doesn't copy from existing sources. You own all generated content and can use it commercially without attribution.
