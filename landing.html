Frequently Asked Questions

How does the AI generate content?

We use advanced language models (GPT-4) trained on high-performing content from each platform. The AI analyzes your input, extracts key points, and restructures them following platform-specific best practices.

Is the generated content original?

Yes! The AI creates unique content based on your input. It doesn't copy from existing sources. You own all generated content and can use it commercially.

Can I edit the generated content?

Absolutely! The AI gives you a strong starting point, but we always recommend reviewing and tweaking the content to match your personal voice.

What languages are supported?

Currently English and Slovak. We're working on adding more languages soon.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes! There are no contracts. You can cancel your subscription at any time and you'll keep access until the end of your billing period.
