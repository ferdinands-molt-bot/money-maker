## Executive Summary

**Company:** ContentMultiplier  
**Product:** AI-powered content repurposing SaaS  
**Mission:** Help creators multiply their content reach without multiplying their work  
**Target Market:** Content creators, marketers, entrepreneurs, agencies  
**Funding Needed:** $0 (bootstrapped)  
**Projected MRR (Month 12):** $15,000-$50,000

## Problem

Content creators spend **70% of their time on distribution** and only **30% on creation**.

Specific pain points:
- 3+ hours per blog post repurposing for social media
- Inconsistent messaging across platforms
- Creator burnout from overwork
- Missed opportunities due to lack of time

**Market Size:**
- 50M+ content creators globally
- Content marketing industry: $400B+ (2026)
- Social media management tools: $20B+ market
